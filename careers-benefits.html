Benefits at foodpanda

Within our diverse community, we know that each of us has different lifestyles and preferences.

That is why we are dedicated to bringing our full time pandas a suite of inclusive and flexible benefits that support a fulfilling experience at foodpanda.

Health and wellness

Helping you be in the best of health

  • Medical and dental coverage
  • Health screening
  • Flexible benefits
  • Access to mental health apps and employee assistance program

Time off and flexibility

Time off when you need it

  • Annual leave
  • Sick leave
  • Marriage leave
  • Birthday leave
  • Compassionate leave
  • Hybrid working arrangement

Family care and support

Caring for your loved ones

  • Family care leave / casual leave
  • Maternity leave
  • Paternity leave
  • Life and accident insurance for employees

Please note that while our philosophy is the same in every market where foodpanda is present, benefits may vary by market.
